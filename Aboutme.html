About me






I am a Junior at the University of California, San Diego and Freie Universität Berlin , majoring in Psychology.

I spend most of my days and nights learning about the intriguing puzzle of the human mind.


I would like to devote my work to improving the well-being of people, by conducting research in the field of Psychology and Cognitive Neuroscience!
